Audio Gallery

Audio refers to sound that can be heard by the human ear, typically ranging from 20 Hz to 20,000 Hz. In technology, audio is captured using microphones, converted into digital signals, and stored as files like MP3, WAV, or AAC. These files can be played back through speakers or headphones using sound cards or embedded systems. Audio plays a vital role in communication, entertainment, education, and accessibility—from podcasts and music to screen readers and voice assistants
