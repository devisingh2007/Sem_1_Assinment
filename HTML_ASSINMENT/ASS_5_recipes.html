Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa

- Rooted in South India, dosa itself dates back over 1,500 years, with early mentions in Tamil literature like Silappatikaram and Sangam poetry

- The masala dosa, with its spiced potato filling, is believed to have originated in the Udupi region of Karnataka in the 1930s

Ingredients:

  • Idli rice or parboiled rice
  • Urad dal (split black gram)
  • Chana dal (optional, for crispiness)
  • Fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Water
  • Poha (flattened rice)
  • Salt
  • Potato Masala
    1. - Boiled potatoes
    2. - Onions
    3. - Green chilies
    4. - Ginger
    5. Curry leaves
    6. - Mustard seeds
    7. - Turmeric powder

Cooking Steps:

  1. Heat Tawa
  2. Spread Batter
  3. Drizzle Oil or Ghee
  4. Cook Until Crisp
  5. Add Masala & Fold
